Chess

I began playing chess during the pandemic and have since fallen in love with it! I have been the captain of the Dover-Sherborn chess team for 3 years now and I'm excited to see new generations of chess players every year!

Band (Trumpet!)

Over the years I have become increasingly involved in my school's band program, moving on from just being a curious trumpet student to now being involved in pep rallies, Memorial Day celebrations, graduation performances, and even jazz band.

Skiing

Skiing is my favorite winter activity, especially when I'm with family and friends.

Mountain Biking

Mountain Biking is my favorite summer activity. Some of my coolest interactions with nature have been during early morning mountain biking trips when bears, owls, and other creatures are still roaming the woods.

Web Design

Robotics

I got started with robotics in my Junior year in high school. Through my experience with the team I have learned a lot about leadership, fundraising, and the engineering design process. If you are interested robotics, I encourage you to connect with a local FIRST robotics group.
